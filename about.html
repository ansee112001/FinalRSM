About

AR has brought significant transformations to various fields, one of which is social media. Social media is a means communication, information and entertainment that cannot be separated from our lives. For friends who may not know what AR is. So AR itself technology that combines two-dimensional and / or three-dimensional virtual objects into a real environment and then projects those virtual objects in real time. This AR technology can enter certain information into cyberspace and display it in the real world with the help of equipment such as webcams, computers, Android phones, and special glasses. So in this AR social media itself I use a webcam, where apart from taking photos it also provides features that will amaze anyone. Also through this webcam, we can try something we want to buy. for example we want to buy clothes, then by using AR technology in the form of a webcam, we will be able to measure or try on the clothes as if we are wearing them because as explained AR combines the virtual world with real life. After it feels right, then friends make a purchase. Likewise, when friends want to buy makeup, pants or dresses, you can try them on first and then make a purchase if you like or are interested in buying them. It feels like being tough, so you can try it first. That's why I want to share something about this world of technology with friends and of course this will be very interesting!
